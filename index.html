About Our College

Shah and Anchor Kutchhi Engineering College was established in 1985 for the purpose of imparting quality technical education. The college is managed by Mahavir Education Trust. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and Government of Maharashtra, and is affiliated to University of Mumbai. It also has an ISO 9001:2015 Certification. It offers undergraduate courses in Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Computer Science, Electronics & Telecommunication, Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Cyber Security, VLSI Design & Technology and Advance Communication Technology. It also offers postgraduate courses in Computer Engineering, Information Technology and Electronics Engineering. UG Program in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering is accredited by NBA. The institute is accredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC and has been granted autonomy by the University Grants Commission (UGC) from the academic year 2024–25 to 2033–34.
